INSTRUCTION

Three style variations are able for choosing: Modern, Renesans, Gothic. Click on one of them to change the game's appearance.

The top left corner is showing the number of mines in the current game difficulty. When you start a new round there is a stopwatch in the top right corner. In the middle of the top panel, there is a restart button. To start a new game press this button with your mouse or press "R" with your keyboard.

There are three game difficulties: Easy, Medium, Hard. Click on one of them to choose an appropriate difficulty.
